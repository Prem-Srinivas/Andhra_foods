Product Description

A taste of Godavari is never complete without a generous helping of their favorite side dish, Andhra’s fish pickle. Fish Achar is the most popular, traditional, and delicious side dish of the Godavari district. A fish pickle served as a side dish with cooked rice has a tangy and tantalizing flavor, which is because of the unique mix of masala made especially for fish achar and traditional culinary methods employed in processing.
