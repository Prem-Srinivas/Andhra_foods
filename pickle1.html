Product Description

Andhra style Avakaya pachadi / Mango Pickle is made with high-quality freshly bought mangoes, which are best for pickle preparation. This Mango Pickle is famous in overall India with various unique names like bedekar pickle, Kadumanga achar, Kannimanga achar, and gorkeri pickle, etc. No home in Andhra will not pass summer without tasting the flavors of Avakaya or Aam Ka Achar. This pickle was made with the authentic Godavari recipe, which is a simple feast for long-lasting Avakaya memories. Ingredients Mangoes Fenugreek seeds Mustard seeds Garlic Ground nut Oil Salt Red Chili Powder
