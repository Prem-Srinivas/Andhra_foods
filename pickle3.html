Product Description

Tomato pickle is one of the famous side dishes for breakfast time in all the households of India. This tomato nilva pachadi is relished and identified with different names such as thakkali oorugai or tamatar achar in various areas. Tomato nilva pachadi is made of freshly ripened tomatoes and pure and natural ingredients, adding no additional artificial flavors to let you enjoy the genuineness of cuisine.
